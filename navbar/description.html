Description du produit

At Crowd Cookware, we’ve been working on chef-grade cookware with the goal of transforming the cookware industry. With our Tasman we’ll be taking this transformation to a new level of innovation. By producing a recycled Dutch Oven & Grill without digging one single piece of iron.

The Dutch Oven & the Grill can both be used on all heat sources including gas, induction, ceramic and electric. It can even be put in the oven or on the barbeque.

The Tasman is dishwasher safe, though handwashing is recommended to last for generations. It is easy to clean, most messes just need a gentle soak and scrub for a smooth finish.
